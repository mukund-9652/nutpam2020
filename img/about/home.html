About


World's First & Virtual NUTPAM 2020!!

Bounding the theme 'ANYONE CAN CODE', Sri Sairam Institute of Technology is hosting a one-day Tech Fest virtually to bring out talent's among budding engineers. It becomes interesting when code meets fun and when it happens from your own placeholder!!!

Going Virtual


For the safety of the 1000+ people involved at the yearly event, NUTPAM 2020 has decided to move completely online for the 2020 season. We will still offer sponsor challenges, awesome prizes, fun games and more! See you on 29th October!

EVENTS

EVENTS
Tchnowar Technovation BATTLE OF DESIGNS GRAB THE INFINITIES Scholar Photography Gaming Contest CONNEC"T"ECH

TechnoWar

Description:
  • The participants will be given a question with 3/4 th of the code (python preferably).
  • The participant has to read and understand the code and then solve it to get the workable solution.
  • Individual participation.

Technovation

Description:
  • The participants will be given problem statements from StudyMonk.
  • The participants must solve the problem within the given time.
  • Minimum 2 and maximum 3 persons are allowed

Battle Of Design

Description:
  • Exclusive event for designers and meme creators.
  • The participants will be given a page with a name of any app eg. Home page, login page.
  • The participant has to design a template with their own design in any photo editing software.
  • Individual participation.

Grab The Infinities

Description:
  • This technical event consists of 6 different programs and where each of the programs are interlinked to each other.
  • Initially the coder starts with a problem and if solves a problem then he will get a string output along with the next program he/she has to solve.
  • The clue or the solutions from the initial program is given as a test/custom input by the user to next program to get the next clue and next program he has to solve.
    So, this process continues until he solves the final program (6th one) and got the very last result revealed to the moderators.
  • The fastest hand wins the game.
  • All the coders must have "HackerRank "account and all the languages in HackerRank are allowed.
  • Individual participation.

Scholar's Tale

Description:
  • The participants have to present their projects
  • Topics not restricted
  • Maximum 2 persons are allowed

Photography

Description:
  • Taking an image, freezing the moment reveals how rich reality is.
  • Take a good picture, send us your picture, we will post it on our Instagram page and those who will get the more likes will be awarded as winners and as runners.
  • Individual participation.

Online Gaming

Description:
  • Multiplayer battle of 3vs3 or battle of 6 vs 6(based on the count we will decide the battle on the spot)
  • The Team with Highest kill count qualifies for the Next Round. Show your Mobile Gaming Skills and Win the Tournament!!

CONNEC"T"ECH

Description:
  • Connection game is a type of abstract strategy game in which the set of pictures will be projected to the players.
  • Players attempt to complete the specific type of connection with the pictures and should come up with the correct collaborated words.
  • The First person who answered the most will be awarded.
  • Individual participation.

FAQs

What is a Nutpam 2020?

It is a one day symposium conducted by the students of Department Computer Science and Engineering of Sri Sairam Institute of Technology.

Who can come?

Nutpam 2020 is open to any enrolled undergraduate or graduate student.

When is the event?

October 29, 2020!!!!

How does registration work?

Where is the event?

For the health and safety of our participants and , Nutpam 2020 will be held online.
